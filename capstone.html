Capstone Project

For this project I needed to research on how to increase profit of Unicorn company, because recently company started to lose more money.

I started from finding most unprofitable states that have company’s services, as you can see on the map, state with the biggest money loss is Texas (red color) with almost 26,000-dollar wastage, next go Ohio and Pennsylvania, with not so bad but still big losses.

After some data research I found out that profit drop is correlated with discount value (Table n1). If you look on the table you see that in Texas, across all years, in Furniture and Office Supply category have losses with average discount up to 40% while technology category has profit with up to 20% discounts.

If we now take most profitable state New York for comparison average discount for all categories didn’t extend 11%, with huge profit compare to Texas.

For more incites you can observe this table with comparison of average discount and profit for all subcategories (Table n2).

In the end I suggest to overview Graph n1 and Graph n2. You can see here that when discount start to rise, profit is declined and vice versa.

While discount is good for attract new clients and encourage or regular customers to buy more, I strongly recommend to cut discounts for more profit, no need to refuse from them but make fewer special offers like this in the future.
